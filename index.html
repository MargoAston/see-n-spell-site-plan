Overview

Purpose

See -n- Spell is an online spelling game for 1st graders. The purpose of the game is to guide the student through the 'sounding-out' process of spelling simple words. The student is guided in identifying the targeted spelling pattern. The student is then guided to identify the consonant letters. Finally the student's knowledge is assesed when asked to spell the complete word. The score is displayed dynamically on a graph. The student should have phonemic awareness (words are made up of individual sounds) and basic phonics knowledge (each letter has a corresponding sound.)

Target Audience

Description

Our target audience is made up of children in the 1st grade, their parents, and teachers. Typically the child is 6 or 7 years old. The child and parent are looking for a fast, easy way to practice spelling words. The teacher is looking for an effective spelling tool that focuses on spelling patterns and engages the student.
